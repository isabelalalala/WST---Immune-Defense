Gameplay Guide

How to Play

01 — Threat
🦠

Know Your Enemy

Six pathogen types invade across five arterial lanes — from fast fragile viruses to the devastating Prion. Each has distinct speed, HP, and damage.

02 — Placement
🛡️

Deploy Defenders

Select a cell card from the HUD and click an empty grid tile to place it. Eight immune cell types offer unique roles — shooters, devourers, mines, and more.

03 — Economy
⚡

Manage ATP

Stem Cells generate ATP drops — click them to collect. Defeating pathogens earns bonus ATP. Spend wisely; over-committing early leaves you exposed later.

04 — Strategy
🔥

Avoid Inflammation

Stack 3+ defenders in one lane and it ignites — pathogens slow down, but Stem Cell output halves. Spread your forces or pay the metabolic price.
