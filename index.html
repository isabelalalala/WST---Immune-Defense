How to Play

🎯

Goal

Defend your body from pathogens by placing immune cells across 5 artery lanes. Stop them reaching the left edge.

⚡

ATP Currency

Your resource — earned over time and by defeating pathogens. Click ATP drops on the field to collect them!

💉

Inject

Remove a placed defender to recover half its ATP cost. Reposition strategically as threats evolve.

🔥

Inflammation

Place 3+ defenders in one lane — pathogens slow, but Stem Cells generate ATP at half speed. Plan wisely.

01

Waves grow harder each round — use your ATP wisely and avoid over-spending early.

02

Mix Stem Cells for economy with offensive cells like Neutrophils and Eosinophils for balance.

03

Platelets ignite an entire lane — save them for dense waves for maximum payoff.

04

Watch out for Prion — 600 HP means it needs concentrated firepower from multiple cells.

🟢 Normal

The intended first-time experience. Balanced ATP economy and forgiving enemy stats let you experiment with different defender strategies and learn the inflammation mechanic at your own pace.

🟡 Hard

Pathogens are noticeably tankier and faster, rewards are reduced, and enemies spawn more frequently with one additional unit per group. ATP is tighter — efficient placement matters. Recommended after clearing Normal.

🔴 Brutal

A ruthless gauntlet. Enemies hit nearly twice as hard, move 50% faster, and arrive in large groups with compressed spawn timers. ATP starts at just 90 and rewards are drastically cut. Every decision must be deliberate.
